              Q & A

              When and where you began playing, and why this sport?
              "I started the sport in primary school. When I was a little girl, I accompanied my parents to go the badminton court and naturally love this sport!"

              Your equipment sponsor and current racket model?
              "Victor, TI99"

              Memorable sporting achievements?
              "2011 Yonex OCBC US Open Grand Prix Gold Champion, 2010 Li-Ning Singapore Open (World Superseries) - it's my first World Superseries final and the final was my birthday, the audiences collectively gave me their wishes when I entered the court. That is the most memorable experience I have in the sport so far."

              Sporting awards / Honours (so far)?
              "The Elite Athletes Award 2011 - The best rookie player"

              Sporting ambitions?
              "To win an Olympic medal"
